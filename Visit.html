HOW IT WORKS

TELL US ABOUT YOURSELF - When you book your appointment online, you'll be asked a few short questions about your project, your goals, and your style. This is to get a better sense of your project so that we can match you with the perfect designer for your needs.

MEET WITH ONE OF OUR DESIGNERS - You'll meet with your designer for a complimentary, one hour personal consultation and begin to discuss your needs and goals. The more you have to share with us at this point, the better. Bring pictures of your room or of things that inspire you, samples, any measurements you have, or any things you are coveting.

MAKE A PLAN - Your designer will help you to develop a plan of action. A layout of the room will be provided, suitable options for furniture and decor may be suggested, and an overall design direction will be discovered. You're under no obligation to buy, but should you choose to, we'll assist you with that.

HOURS AND DIRECTIONS

Modern Relik is open Monday - Saturday, 10-5. Summer Hours: Monday - Friday 10-5

Directions:

From 95 (Route 128): Exit 95 at exit 27A or 27B depending on which direction you are heading. Turn onto Totten Pond Rd East, towards Waltham. Turn right onto Lexington Street (slight right, not sharp right). Turn left on to Beaver Street. At rotary, use 3rd exit and stay on Beaver Street. Continue straight, past Bentley College, and through the main intersection, over the train tracks. Turn left onto Clematis Ave. Modern Relik is about halfway down the street in a brick building on the left.

From I90 (Mass Pike): Exit 17 towards Newton/Watertown. Negotiate the rotary until you find yourself on Galen Street towards Watertown Center. When you pass over the river stay in the left lane and turn left onto Main St. Continue 1/2 a mile, and take a right on Waverley Ave. After .3 miles, take a left on Warren Street. Continue to the end of the street. Take a right on Warren St. Then take a left on Beaver Street. Then take a right on Clematis Ave. Modern Relik is about halfway down the street in a brick building on the left.

From Route 2: Exit on the Pleasant Street exit (59). Turn onto Pleasant Street towards Belmont Center. Continue on Pleasant St for 2 miles. Then take a sharp left on Trapelo Rd. Take your first right on Lexington Street. Then take your second right onto Sycamore St. Continue to the end of the road, and take a left on Belmont Street. At the fork in the road, after .3 miles, stay right onto Beaver Street. Take your second right onto Clematis Ave. Modern Relik is about halfway down the street in a brick building on the left.
