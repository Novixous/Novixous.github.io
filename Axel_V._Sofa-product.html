AXEL V. SOFA

$2,985.00
View details
    ABOUT

    The Axel V. pays homage to Belgian designer Axel Vervoordt's timeless interior designs. Nothing creates a sleek contemporary look like the low track arms of this long, roomy sofa. A single bench cushion seat and minimal adornment help to create unbroken lines and plains that are understated, lean, and confident. This is a transitional sofa that works beautifully in spaces that can't go too modern but beg a stylish look. Available in standard or extra long length as well as a queen sleeper size.

    Standard cushions are semi-firm with a bounce-back appearance and down back cushions. Cushions can be modified for additional softness. Constructed of exceptional craftsmanship, the frame is made of hardwood with double-doweled joints, and houses eight-way, hand tied spring construction.

    Photos below show slipcovered version. Upholstered version will have tight fabric.
